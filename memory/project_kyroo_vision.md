Kyroo is an AI-powered personal fitness coaching app — "your personal fitness coach in your pocket."

**Core value proposition:**
- Fully personalized training plans generated by AI (not generic, one-size-fits-all)
- User answers questions about fitness level, preferences, and goals → AI builds a custom plan
- Ongoing motivation, guidance, and structure — not just plan generation

**Why:** Damian's mission is to help people reach their fitness goals, feel great, and unlock the best version of themselves. This is a deeply personal project born from a lifelong passion for sports and fitness.

**How to apply:** When suggesting features or UX decisions, prioritize personalization and the "coach in your pocket" feeling. Avoid anything that feels generic or impersonal. The brand voice is motivating and human.
